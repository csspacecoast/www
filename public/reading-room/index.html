Christian Science Reading Room

Our Christian Science Reading Room has both a bookstore and a reading area for the exploration of spiritual healing, prayer, and spirituality. Visitors are welcome to ask questions about Christian Science, read and study on site, and make purchases.

Merchandise for purchase includes:

  • Bibles and Bible study resources
  • Science and Health with Key to the Scriptures
  • Other writings by Mary Baker Eddy
  • Music and inspirational CDs
  • Weekly, monthly and quarterly Christian Science publications
  • The Christian Science Monitor
